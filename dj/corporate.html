BEYOND PARTY: Disc Jockey will create a 3 hour energy filled musical extravaganza using the latest in sound technology. Our personnel are well versed in providing the perfect mix of music, playing the right songs at the right times. Package includes:
  • Professional Speakers
  • Amplifier System
  • Audio software with over 20,000 songs
  • 2 Microphones for announcements
COST: $369.99 back to top

NHANCED PARTY: Package includes all items listed under the Beyond Party:
  • Professional Speakers
  • Amplifier System
  • Audio software with over 20,000 songs
  • 2 Microphones for announcements
plus your choice of either of the following options:
  • an American Idol Style Contest is coordinated byan MC host using our Karaoke software with over 10,000 songs available to bring out the talent in your guests. Karaoke from the Japanese meaning 'empty orchestra' is a form of interactive entertainment in which individual(s) sing along with recorded music. A winner is declared and awarded the grand prize. (extra microphones can be provided)
  • a Jeopardy Style Interactive Contest is coordinated by an MC host as he leads teams (or single players) against each other in this musically related competition. Prize(s) are awarded for the team (or single player) achieving the highest score.
COST: $449.99 back to top

XTREME PARTY: Package includes all items listed under the Beyond Party:
  • Professional Speakers
  • Amplifier System
  • Audio software with over 20,000 songs
  • 2 Microphones for announcements
plus a Customized Jeopardy Style Interactive Contest is coordinated by an MC host as he leads teams (or single players) against each other in this competition. Questions about the company and perosnnel are submitted to us in 6 categories and we customize the game around them. Great way of building team work and spirit as everyone learns facts about the company and co-workers they might not have already known. Prize(s) are awarded for the team (or single player) achieving the highest score. COST: 549.99 back to top
