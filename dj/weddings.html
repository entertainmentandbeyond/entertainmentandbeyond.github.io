Weddings

Your wedding day will be something you cherish forever. Our party planners, DJ's and Entertainment Party Hosts go Beyond your expectations! We will customize each activity at your reception to make your wedding experience unforgettable. We offer three levels of packages that reflect various levels of entertainment, and budgets. We go Beyond other DJ Companies by including 5 hours of service in all our wedding packages.

Each package features an increased level of involvement with our DJs and Entertainment Party Hosts.

The wedding packages available are: Beyond, Nhanced & Xtreme

BEYOND RECEPTION:

Our Beyond Wedding Reception is ideal for informal Receptions. Our State-of-the-Art Sound System will deliver high quality sound as your Disc Jockey plays your favorite music during your reception while taking requests from your guests and yourself.

Our special effect lighting creates the perfect mood for your dance floor. We use LED sound-activated lights that emit multi-colored beams that rotate back and forth to the beat of the music. Lighting is included as part of all our wedding packages. Wireless and wired microphones are available for toasting and/or general announcements. Package Includes:
  • Disc Jockey
  • 5 Hours of service
  • State-of-the-Art Sound System
  • LED Lighting System with Multi-Color Dance Lighting
  • Wireless and wired microphones
COST: $649.99 back to top

NHANCED RECEPTION: Our Nhanced Wedding Reception is recommended when your celebration needs your DJ service to play an active role in its coordination and choreography. The absolute best way to assure your Reception receives a more interactive service is to provide two entertainers for your event, a Disc Jockey/Music Programmer and a Master of Ceremonies/MC Host. While the Disc Jockey focuses on the music, requests and program changes, the Master of Ceremonies has his attention on the details and flow of your Reception. The entire event will be coordinated by the Master of Ceremonies from the initial introductions to the final goodbye. Your Reception will run smoothly since your MC works closely with you, your bridal party, Catering Manager, and Photographer. The Master of Ceremonies will also interact with your guests, motivating them onto the dance floor to partake in all the fun. As in our Beyond Wedding Reception package, our State-of-the-Art Sound system and special lighting deliver high quality sound and the perfect ambiance to the reception. Package Includes:
  • Disc Jockey
  • Master of Ceremonies
  • Complete Coordination of Details
  • 5 Hours of service
  • State-of-the-Art Sound System
  • LED Lighting System with Multi-Color Dance Lighting
  • Wireless and wired microphones
COST: $849.99 back to top

XTREME RECEPTION: Our Xtreme Wedding Reception provides all of the services included in the Nhanced Reception and adds the following enhancements to make sure you and your guests have a memorable evening.
  1. Visualization Show of Bride and Groom - A custom picture show, created from your treasured photographs that can be shown during the Cocktail Hour, Dinner Hour, or throughout the entire Reception. This service enables you to share with your guests the special memories of your courtship that brought you to this special day, adding a personal touch that will make your Reception unforgettable.
  2. Music Visualization - A visualization show of laser lights projected on a screen that complements both the music and lighting.
  3. Xtreme Lighting - 3 LED lighting systems, including a Strobe on a truss system.
  4. A second Music System - Located in the cocktail area, if separate from the reception area.
As in our Wedding Reception packages, our State-of-the-Art Sound system and special lighting deliver high quality sound and the perfect ambiance to the reception. Package Includes:
