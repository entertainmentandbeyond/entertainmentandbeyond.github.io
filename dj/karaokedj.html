DJ/Karaoke

Karaoke (bimoraic clipped compound of Japanese kara "empty" and ōkesutora "orchestra"), Japanese: [kaɽaoke]is a form of interactive entertainment or video game in which amateur singers sing along with recorded music (a music video) using a microphone and public address system. The music is typically a well-known pop song minus the lead vocal. Lyrics are usually displayed on a video screen, along with a moving symbol, changing color, or music video images, to guide the singer. It is also a term used by recording engineers translated as "empty track" meaning there is no vocal track.
