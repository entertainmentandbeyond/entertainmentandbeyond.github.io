Bar/Bat Mitzvahs

The absolute best way to assure your Special Event receives a more interactive service is to provide two entertainers for your event, a Disc Jockey/Music Programmer and a Master of Ceremonies/MC Host. While the Disc Jockey focuses on the music, requests and program changes, the Master of Ceremonies has his attention on the details and flow of your party. The entire event will be coordinated by the Master of Ceremonies from the initial introductions to the final goodbye. Your party will run smoothly since your MC works closely with you, Catering Manager, and Photographer. The Master of Ceremonies will also interact with your guests, motivating them onto the dance floor to partake in all the fun. Our State-of-the-Art Sound system and special lighting deliver high quality sound and the perfect ambiance to your party.
