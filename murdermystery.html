Murder Mysteries

What a perfect and unique idea for your next dinner party, birthday celebration or corporate event.

It's interactive as you and your guests atempt to solve a ficticious murder that has occurred at the party.

We can do everything to facilitate your murder mystery party. We will even send out the invitations to your guests. There are no scripts to read, with the exception of the Evidence Presentation and the Solution. Our interactive mingle format allows more intimacy between guests. You become a part of the mystery, instead of feeling like you are just sitting around a table reading cards and playing a game. No more dreaded party lulls! Be prepared to discover how cunning your friends REALLY are!

We offer murder mystery party game kits to fit all your needs for both Adults and Children. Parties are available for parties of 8 to 80 participants.

2 types of party packages are available: Beyond & Nhanced

BEYOND PARTY: Our party host conducts the entire murder mystery and...
  • sends out guest invitations
  • assigns character parts
  • provides scripts
  • coordinates acts/events
  • provides props for up to 20 guests
  • awards prizes
