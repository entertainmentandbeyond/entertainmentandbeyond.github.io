4 X 6 PHOTO PRINTS - Increase the size from a 2x6 strip to a 4x6 print with 4 pictures.
LIVE PROJECTION OF PHOTOS ON BIG SCREEN - Photos taken in the booth are immediately projected on a large screen for viewing.
